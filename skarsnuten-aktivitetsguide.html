Rett utenfor døren
Ski in / Ski out

⛷️ Hemsedal Skisenter

Norges nest største alpinsenter med 51 løyper fordelt på tre fjelltopper. Spenn på deg skiene utenfor døren og gli ned Alpinstien (løype 47) rett inn i heissystemet. Tilbake opp med Skarsnutenheisen.

Skipass kjøpes på skistar.com – spar tid og unngå kø.

Right outside your door
Ski in / Ski out

⛷️ Hemsedal Ski Resort

Norway's second largest ski resort with 51 runs across three peaks. Click into your bindings outside the door and glide down Alpinstien (run 47) straight into the lift system. Return via Skarsnutenheisen.

Lift passes at skistar.com – save time by buying ahead.

All levels

🧒 Valle Kids' Area

Perfect for little ones! Valle Snowland has safe button lifts, magic carpets and fun features. At the base area – easily reached by ski or free ski bus.

Hiking
Into the mountains

🥾 Top 20 Mountain Hikes

20 marked summits from easy stroller-friendly trails to challenging black-rated peaks. Collect pin badges at the tourist office!

All levels

🧗 Via Ferrata

Climb safely along the cliff at Skarsnuten with steel cables and ladders. Panoramic views! Book via Hemsedal Aktiv.

Adults / teens

🚴 Mountain biking

Tottelia Rides has purpose-built trails in three difficulty levels. Bike rental in the centre and at the ski resort. E-bikes also popular!

All ages

🌊 River snorkelling in Hemsila

Float downstream and meet trout face-to-face! One of Norway's most unique nature experiences.

Adventurous

🌲 Høyt & Lavt Climbing Park

Treetop courses for all levels including a zipline across the river. A guaranteed hit with families.

Families

🐴 Horseback riding

Guided mountain rides with Hemsedal Western Horses or Skogshorn Riding Camp.

All ages

🎣 Fishing

The Hemsila river is one of Norway's best for trout fishing. Permits available locally.

All ages
💡 Host's tip

Check hemsedal.com for updated activity calendar and booking.

Restauranter & barer

Vår nærmeste nabo, kun 500 m unna. Nordisk eleganse møter italiensk lidenskap. Fantastisk middag med panoramautsikt, eller drinker på den store uteterrassen.
